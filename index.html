Money is something that we use every day to buy things we need or want. It comes in different forms like coins and bills, and it can be in different amounts. Sometimes we have a lot of money and sometimes we have very little. A way to help us keep track of our money is by using a bank. A bank is a safe place where we can keep our money and even earn more money by saving it there. Another important thing about money is that we can also use it to make more money by investing. Investing is when we put our money into something like a business or a stock and it can grow over time. This is like planting a seed and watching it grow into a big tree. It's important to learn about money and how to use it wisely so that we can have enough to buy what we need and save for our future.
